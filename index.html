About Me

Hello, I'm RD.

I am a passionate photographer based in the vibrant city of Rajkot, Gujarat. For over a decade, I've been driven by a single goal: to capture the genuine emotions and fleeting moments that make life so beautiful.

My style is a blend of modern photojournalism and fine-art portraiture. I believe in telling stories through my lens, creating images that are not just seen, but felt. Whether it's the tearful joy of a wedding or the quiet majesty of a mountain peak, I strive to create art that is both authentic and timeless.

Let's connect and create something beautiful together.
